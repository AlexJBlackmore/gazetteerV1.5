Gazetteer

How it works:

  • When prompted, allow location services
  • Pan to a country using the selector in the header
  • Position the center of the map over your location of choice and then tap the buttons on the left
  • Nearby earthquakes, points of interest, and Wikipedia articles will be plotted on the map. Be patient with the Wiki button, it can take some time
  • Click anywhere on the map to see weather info at that location
